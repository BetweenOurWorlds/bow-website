Support us

Between our Worlds is an initiative created to support the publication of anime metadata as Linked Open Data. Therefore, we are open to suggestions, remarks, and contributions, whether you are a developer or not. You can find our code on GitHub and we hang out on Gitter for a virtual coffee and a chat. It's also possible to buy us a real cup of coffee. This will help us to keep our caffeine levels high when we are working on the server.
